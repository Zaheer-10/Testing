About PaperMate

Welcome to PaperMate!

The ultimate machine learning (NLP) project that helps you find, read, and enjoy the best papers in your field. Forget about wasting time on searching - PaperMate knows exactly what you need!

PaperMate has a simple and elegant home page where you can enter your topic of interest using text or voice input. You can also check out the newest arXiv papers on machine learning, NLP, AI, and computer vision, sorted by different categories. PaperMate also explains how it uses smart algorithms to recommend the most suitable papers for you.

When you type or say your topic of interest, PaperMate will show you four papers that match your query. For each paper, you can see the abstract and use four handy features:

  • Read full paper: This button will take you to the original arXiv paper where you can read it from start to finish.
  • Summarize here: This button will create a short summary of the paper for you. It may take a few moments if the paper summary is not already in our database, otherwise it will show up right away.
  • Take a look at the paper: This button will let you view and explore the paper on PaperMate itself. You can zoom in, scroll, and highlight the parts that catch your eye.
  • Talk to me: This button will lead you to the chat page where you can have a conversation with the paper in a natural and friendly way. PaperMate will answer your questions and give you more insights about the paper.
